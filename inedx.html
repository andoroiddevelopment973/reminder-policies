Reminder – Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Reminder has a zero-tolerance policy against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Our Commitments:

  • Prohibited Content: We strictly prohibit any content, images, text, or activities that promote, depict, or attempt to exploit children sexually.
  • Reporting: Any violation related to CSAE will be reported immediately to relevant authorities, including the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) where applicable.
  • Enforcement: Accounts violating this policy will be suspended or removed permanently. Repeat violations will lead to permanent bans and reporting to legal authorities.
  • User Responsibility: Users are forbidden from uploading, sharing, or encouraging CSAE-related content. If you come across such material, please report it immediately to your-email@example.com.

We stand firmly with global standards to protect children from sexual abuse and exploitation.
